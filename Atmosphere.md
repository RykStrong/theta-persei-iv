# TPIV Atmosphere

**Pressure at Surface:** 1.2 atmospheres (~17.6 psi)  
**Temperature Range:** -50°C to +50°C  
**Breathability:** No (not human-compatible)

---

## Atmospheric Structure

**Troposphere (0-18 km):** Weather layer, temperature decreases with altitude  
**Stratosphere (18-50 km):** Ozone layer protects surface from UV  
**Mesosphere (50-85 km):** Coldest layer  
**Thermosphere (85-600 km):** Auroras occur here  
**Exosphere (>600 km):** Gradual transition to space

## Optical Properties

### Sky Color: Blue-Violet

**Causes:**
- Blue-shifted stellar spectrum (F8V star)
- Rayleigh scattering enhanced by higher pressure
- More violet component than Earth

**At Different Times:**
- **Noon:** Bright blue-violet
- **Sunrise/Sunset:** Deep purple-red
- **Night (Lumna-lit):** Silver-blue

### Clouds & Haze
- Organic haze from methane photochemistry (brownish tint)
- Storm clouds appear purple-gray
- More dramatic coloration than Earth

## Biological Impacts

### Photosynthesis
- Higher CO₂ + higher O₂ = very high efficiency
- Blue-shifted light drives evolution of purple/orange [pigments](Photosynthesis%20and%20Pigments.md) (not green)
- Supports massive autotroph biomass

### Respiration
- 25% oxygen enables efficient metabolism
- Supports complex [cognitive functions](Nervous%20Systems.md)
- Powers high-energy behaviors ([separated behaviors](Separated%20Behaviors%20and%20Exovants.md))
- [Many small lungs](Respiratory%20Systems.md) strategy common

### Climate Regulation
- Greenhouse gases buffer temperature extremes
- Water vapor self-regulates (more at perihelion, less at aphelion)
- Stable liquid water across wide latitude range

---

## Weather Patterns

**More Energetic Than Earth:**
- Higher atmospheric mass drives stronger storms
- Frequent tropical cyclones
- Intense thunderstorms (high oxygen)
- Seasonal monsoons critical for ecosystems

**Global Circulation:**
- Hadley cells similar to Earth
- Jet streams at ~10 km altitude
- 31-hour rotation creates Coriolis effect

---

## Human Habitability

This atmosphere is not human breathable due to SO2 and CO2 levels and the presence of NH3.

This biosphere is Level-5 seperation from earth biota, no interaction is safely possible due to biologic and chemical incompatibilities.

---

## Atmospheric Chemistry

**Key Cycles:**

**Oxygen-Carbon:**
- Photosynthesis produces O₂
- Respiration consumes O₂
- Maintains 25% equilibrium

**Methane:**
- Biological production constant
- UV photolysis breaks down
- Creates organic haze

**Nitrogen:**
- Biological fixation ([Trophic Roles No Plant Animal](Trophic%20Roles%20No%20Plant%20Animal.md))
- Lightning fixation
- Returns via decomposition
