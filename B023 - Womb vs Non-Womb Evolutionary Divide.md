# B023 – Primary Evolutionary Divide in TPIV Biology: Womb vs. Non-Womb Assembly

## Summary

Field data from Magna, Apanon, and Kolofluke strongly suggest that all life on TPIV adheres to one of two fundamental reproductive strategies:

- **Womb-assembled organisms**, in which genetically distinct partifuncts produce partifzygotes that are assembled inside a specialised womb partifunct, and
- **Non-womb-assembled organisms**, in which free-floating or minimally fused GFP bundles wrapped in BLU self-organise into metabolising units directly in the environment.

This distinction may represent the most profound biological division yet observed on TPIV — akin to the difference between sexual and asexual reproduction on Earth, or even between prokaryotic and eukaryotic structures.

---

## I. Womb-Assembled Life

**Mechanism:**
- Each partifunct collects compatible GFP from environmental pools
- Recombines to form a partifzygote
- Partifzygotes are transferred to a womb partifunct, where assembly-conception occurs
- Organisms are gestated with epigenetic instruction from the womb structure
- Composite bodies are metabolically integrated but genetically modular

**Examples from Steppe Biome (Magna):**
- Zebra Grazer
- Pale Sprint Grazer
- Crimson Strider
- Banded Pack Hunter
- Duskmantle Stalker
- Sickle-Maw Pairhunter
- Bronze Crest Diver

**Distributed Organism (DO) Examples (Apanon/Kolofluke):**
- Horse DO-Grazer (unified gestation via rotating reproductive node)
- Drophunter Blimps (prey-drop/guard-eat specialisation)
- River Guardian DO *(dispatch pending)*
- HillWolf DO *(dispatch pending)*

## II. Non-Womb-Assembled Life

**Mechanism:**
- Some GFP carriers shed pre-wrapped in functional BLU
- If compatibility thresholds are met, units self-fuse or activate metabolism directly
- Common among autotrophs, floating phototrophs, and microfeeders
- Developmental pattern is decentralised, fast, and probabilistic

**Examples (Confirmed):**
- Stalk Trees
- Clam Grasses
- Brownflower Shrubs
- Obsidian Mats
- Mass-Crawling Micro-Feeder
- Hopping Micro-Feeder
- Gold-Toned Planktoforms
- Sky Spore-Droplets *(pending dispatch)*

---

## III. Ambiguous or Hybrid Strategies

- Little Brown Bird Feeders — suspected minimal-womb assembly or secondary gestation
- Winterflies and Frostblooms — potential layered reproduction, involving both dispersed modular BLU and womb-alike fusion
- Weaver Grass DO (Apanon) — distributed, decentralised assembly resembling both categories

---

## Significance

This fundamental divide shapes:
- Ecosystem structure and trophic cycling
- Pace and direction of evolutionary change
- Distribution of metabolic and behavioural complexity

Further work is needed to trace partifunct lineages across both categories and to determine whether this divide originated early in evolutionary history or emerged convergently.
