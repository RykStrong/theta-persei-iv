# Theta Persei IV - Xenobiological Survey Wiki

**Welcome to the TPIV Xenobiology Database**

This wiki documents the unique biology, ecology, and planetary characteristics of Theta Persei IV, a terrestrial planet orbiting an F8V star approximately [distance] light-years from Earth. The information compiled here represents the findings of the PRXEMA (Planetary Research Xenogeological Exploration and Mapping Agency) survey project 'Green Horizon', this was mission 'Green Horizon 67'.

### Key Distinctions from Earth Biology

**What Makes TPIV Unique:**
- **No DNA** - Genetic information stored in [Genomic Folded Proteins (GFP)](GFP%20and%20Genodules.md)
- **No traditional cells** - Life based on [Basic Life Units (BLUs)](BLU%20Biology.md) 100x larger than Earth cells
- **No species** - Replaced by statistical [taxa](Taxa%20and%20Metrics.md) defined by partifunct assemblages
- **Modular organisms** - Each creature is an assembly of genetically distinct [partifuncts](Partifunct%20Core%20Definition.md)
- **Environmental reproduction** - Genetic material collected from the environment via [mites, pollen, and vectors](GFP%20Carriers%20and%20Vectors.md)
- **No plant/animal distinction** - All life exists on a [continuum](Trophic%20Roles%20No%20Plant%20Animal.md)
