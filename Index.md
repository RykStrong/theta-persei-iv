# Theta Persei IV - Xenobiological Survey Wiki

**Welcome to the TPIV Xenobiology Database**

This wiki documents the unique biology, ecology, and planetary characteristics of Theta Persei IV, a terrestrial planet orbiting an F8V star approximately [distance] light-years from Earth. The information compiled here represents the findings of the PRXEMA (Planetary Research Xenogeological Exploration and Mapping Agency) survey project 'Green Horizon', this was mission 'Green Horizon 67'.

### Key Distinctions from Earth Biology

**What Makes TPIV Unique:**
- **No DNA** - Genetic information stored in [[GFP-and-Genodules|Genomic Folded Proteins (GFP)]]
- **No traditional cells** - Life based on [[21-BLUs-Basic-Life-Units|Basic Life Units (BLUs)]] 100x larger than Earth cells
- **No species** - Replaced by statistical [[31-Taxa-and-Metrics-PSV-MDI|taxa]] defined by partifunct assemblages
- **Modular organisms** - Each creature is an assembly of genetically distinct [[Partifunct Core Definition|partifuncts]]
- **Environmental reproduction** - Genetic material collected from the environment via [[GFP-Carriers-and-Vectors|mites, pollen, and vectors]]
- **No plant/animal distinction** - All life exists on a [[41-Trophic-Roles-No-Plant-Animal|continuum]]
