## Partifunct Domain Codes

### Standard Classification

**Structural (ST):**
- LMB: Limb segments
- PAD: Foot/pad structures
- GRD: Girdle (hex/octa)
- SKN: Skin/armor

**Internal (IN):**
- DGV: Digestive
- CRC: Circulatory
- MTB: Metabolic

**Respiratory (RS):**
- LUN: Lung systems
- SPR: Spiracle structures

**Cognitive (CN):**
- MOTO: Motor control
- MEMO: Memory/learning
- SOCI: Social interaction
- STRA: Strategic planning
- SENS: Sensory integration
- NAVI: Navigation
- THRT: Threat recognition

**Sensory (SN):**
- VIS: Visual
- AUD: Auditory
- CHM: Chemical
- TCT: Tactile
- RAD: Radiation
- MAG: Magnetic

**Reproductive (WP):**
- WMB: Womb partifunct

**Autotrophy (AT):**
- PHO: Photosynthetic
- NIT: Nitrogen-fixing
- SAP: Saprotrophic

**Electrogenesis (EL):**
- WRD: Wired electricity
- WRL: Wireless electricity
