Midweight plains grazer, steppe biome

Hexapedal, striped dorsal surface, vertical sensory stalk, average adult mass ~500kg, height 2.2m + 1m sensory stalk

### **Partifunct Core Set:**

We are currently tracking the **12–15 most common partifuncts** comprising approximately **90% of organismal mass and metabolic function**. Preliminary scans reveal **dozens more low-volume partifuncts**, likely involved in micro-regulation, immunological stability, micro-vascular tuning, and transient sensory integration. These **minor partifuncts** collectively represent **<10% of total biomass** and are not yet cataloged individually.
